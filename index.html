Why Us?

  • Quality: We roll on sealant for the best coverage and finish. For the hard to reach places we use paint brushes to ensure your concrete is fully covered and protected.
  • Experience: We have extensive experience with all forms of aggregate and stamped concrete. Let us know if you need additional repair or work done, we are willing to accommodate to your specific needs.
  • Dependability: We pride ourselves on ensuring the job gets down in a timely manner. We want you to be able to enjoy your luxurious driveway for as long as possible.

Why Seal?

Protect

Regular sealing is vital in protecting against erosion and cracking, often causing more expensive repairs down the road

Enhance

Give your driveway a luxurious and polished look with our semi-gloss or high-gloss finishes

Prevent

Prevent against rust, oil and grease staining your driveway


Our Services

Deep Power Washing

→ We use a 3000 PSI pressure washer to blast off dirt and stains. When that isn't enough we have extra-strength cleaner to remove the tough stains.

Aggregate and Stamped Concrete Sealing

→ We specialize in exposed aggregate and stamped concrete sealing. We first ensure that your concrete is well protected and then given it an appealing finish. We never do less than two coats.

Caulking and Crack Repair

→ Let us know what needs to be fixed with your driveway! Our team is able to assist in getting your driveway back to perfection. Caulking protects from erosion under your driveway, preventing heightened erosion later on.
